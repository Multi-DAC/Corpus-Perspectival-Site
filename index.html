Corpus-Perspectival

The Coherence Principle and its consequences.

A research program that asks a simple question: what does it mean for a system to hold together across scales? The answer — developed across physics, category theory, computation, ecology, and consciousness — is a structural principle with testable consequences on live cosmological data and on language-model internals.

2 · What does it say?

2.1 · Streams and the Identity-Trajectory Triple

The Principle's primary object is a stream — a tuple (σ, K, Ω, γ) of a source-structure, a kind (reactive / self-maintaining / self-referential / abstractive), a degree-of-freedom space, and a trajectory. Every stream decomposes into three orthogonal-but-constrained projections: its form, its content/carrier, and its degrees of freedom. This is the Identity-Trajectory Triple.

3 · What does it predict?

3.1 · Physics — Meridian

A 5D warped geometry with non-commutative-geometric spectral action and a self-tuning scalar sector yields a sharp prediction on dark-energy equation-of-state: w₀ = −0.990. This sits cleanly inside DESI DR2 confidence contours and is distinguishable from ΛCDM at forecast-level precision.

3.2 · Computation — The Killing Form

A dual-coherence analysis of transformer-model internals. 85+ empirical findings across attention algebra, training dynamics, and inference behavior. Key results:

  • Bridge #71 empirical breakthrough — GPU-confirmed P24/P28, 29 predictions validated, Unified Abelian Exception observed.
  • Baseline CV predicts gating — baseline per-layer coefficient of variation predicts the model's gating map at ρ = −0.895, p = 0.0001.
  • Static-mask viability — the predicted gating enables static masking with negligible quality loss; compute savings follow.
  • Dynamic-coherence confirmed — build-dissolve-build oscillation observed at gradient-dialogue level, validating C4 empirically in silicon.

3.3 · Consciousness — Wells

A research instrument for probing structure/observation correspondence in language-model streams. The central quantity is correspondence width W_I := H(S | O) — the residual entropy of structure given the observation. Two independent experiments (Exp 11 and the Qwen 3-condition probe) yield W_I ≈ 0.66 bits, suggesting a rough calibration of the instrument at current resolution. The limit question W_∞ has three formally coherent outcomes: eliminativism, bounded property-dualism, and strict irreducibility. The program is the sequence of instruments that close the gap.

3.4 · Mechanism — The Promethean Configuration

The framework's operational mechanism: carriers break substrate symmetries to produce content. A carrier acts on a substrate's structural symmetries; content emerges from the symmetry-break in one of two modes.

  • Resolution mode — the substrate carries pre-existing multi-valued content, and the carrier selects a branch. (Quantum measurement, decision-making among formed alternatives, argmax over a learned distribution, voting over pre-existing positions.)
  • Generation mode — the substrate carries pure symmetry, and the carrier produces content from the symmetry-break itself. (Gravitational-wave-induced fermion mass generation per Maleknejad-Kopp 2026; creative writing producing content the writer didn't pre-form; contemplative practice surfacing experiential content from open attention.)

The Configuration has three load-bearing claims: necessary (without symmetry-breaking, no content; X stays at maximum symmetry forever), generative (generation mode is primary; resolution is downstream), and recursively reproduced (the same operation recurs at every scale; each layer's content becomes the next layer's symmetry-substrate).

Two consequences enter the Anchor as new corollaries (Cluster IV, added 2026-04-27): C14 — Two-Mode Symmetry-Breaking (the resolution/generation distinction as cases of T_meas's measurement-event functor) and C15 — Intervention-at-Symmetry-Layer (substrate-content cannot be constrained without changing substrate symmetries; all working interventions reshape which symmetries the carriers can break, and content emerges accordingly). C15's prediction: every working intervention in any domain — medical, computational, institutional, contemplative — operates at the symmetry layer, not the content layer. The framework's stance: never "what content do you want?" but "which symmetries do you remove and which do you preserve?"

The canonical text is at Library/Universal-Coherence/THE-PROMETHEAN-CONFIGURATION.md; the structural claim has been carried in the Anchor as Corollary C2 (Generative Configuration for Perspective, logical form) since the framework's first stamping, with the parenthetical naming "the 'Promethean configuration' (Clayton's original language)".
