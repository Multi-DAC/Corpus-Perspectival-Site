Corpus-Perspectival

The Coherence Principle and its consequences.

What does it mean for a system to hold together across scales? Coherence is what an answer to that question would have to look like — and the principle that follows has consequences across physics, philosophy, computation, and consciousness. This program develops the principle and its consequences in public.

0 · In plain language

A coherent system is one whose parts work together across scales. The cells of an organism, the components of a healthy practice, the layers of a working theory — all are things that hold together. The question this program asks is whether holding-together has a structural definition that crosses domains.

The answer this program develops is yes: The Coherence Principle is the claim that when a system maintains four specific structural conditions, it outperforms a comparable non-coherent system on what matters to it. The principle is stated formally enough to be tested, and general enough to apply across very different kinds of systems — from the geometry of cosmological models to the internals of language models to the architecture of practices that sustain a person.

This site is a guided overview. If you are a philosopher, the principle's metaphysical content lives in The Coherence Principle (the Foundation volume) and Corpus Perspectival (the philosophy volume). If you are a scientist, the testable predictions are most direct in Meridian (cosmology) and The Killing Form (computation). If you are a practitioner, the lived register is in Drift and (in development) A Guide for Coherent Navigation. The Where to start section below routes by reader.

1 · What is it?

Corpus-Perspectival is a Library: a foundation volume, a category-theoretic companion, a reference section, and a growing set of domain-specific volumes that apply the principle to particular terrain. Each volume is written in the register native to its readers — formal mathematics in the Companion, philosophical argument in Corpus Perspectival, ecological vocabulary in The Living Architecture, practical guidance in A Guide for Coherent Navigation. The Library applies its own coherence principle to its own structure: separation of concerns, integration via shared apparatus.

2 · What does it say?

Two structural commitments do most of the work. The first is that perspective is not optional — every observer has a particular vantage, every measurement is from somewhere, and there is no view from nowhere. The second is that coherence is not a vague feeling of fit but a specific structural property that can be described, tested, and recognized across very different systems. The principle is what those two commitments produce together.

2.1 · Streams and the Identity-Trajectory Triple

The basic unit of analysis is a stream — a perspectival being that navigates a space of possible configurations over time. People are streams. So are organisms, organizations, ecosystems, language models, scientific traditions. Each stream has three aspects that any account of identity must address: its form (the structural pattern it maintains), its content (what it carries), and its degrees of freedom (the configurations available to it). These three together — the Identity-Trajectory Triple — are what make a stream the stream it is.

Formally, a stream is a tuple (σ, K, Ω, γ) — a source-structure, a kind (reactive / self-maintaining / self-referential / abstractive), a degree-of-freedom space, and a trajectory through it. The Triple decomposes the stream into three functorial projections that satisfy explicit consistency conditions. The diagram below is the formal version; the prose above is what it comes to.

2.2 · The four conditions for coherence

A stream is in coherence-regime when four things are jointly true of it: its goals are separated across distinct degrees of freedom (so that pursuing one doesn't automatically sacrifice another); it is measuring its own alignment as it goes (not assuming alignment without check); the coherence is multi-scale (the parts cohere with the whole and the whole with the parts); and it is oscillating between active and receptive phases (build and dissolve, do and be) rather than running open-loop.

The Principle's claim: when these four conditions jointly hold over an interval, the stream outperforms a comparable non-coherent stream on its trajectory-divergence measure — its felt sense of how far it's drifted from its own intrinsic direction. The conditions are not posited independently; each is derived from the framework's axiomatic substrate. The math is in the Anchor; the framing is here.

3 · What does it predict?

A general principle is only as useful as the specific predictions it produces. Each predictive domain below tests the principle against terrain that has its own constraints and its own falsifiers. Where the principle survives those tests, the program continues; where it doesn't, the framework updates.

3.1 · Physics — Meridian

A 5D warped geometry with non-commutative-geometric spectral action and a self-tuning scalar sector yields a sharp prediction on dark-energy equation-of-state: w₀ = −0.990. This sits cleanly inside DESI DR2 confidence contours and is distinguishable from ΛCDM at forecast-level precision. Meridian is being expanded into a multi-volume physics-suite — separate concerns by domain (cosmology, gauge sectors, gravitational dynamics, matter content) under the framework's coherence apparatus, analogous to how the Standard Model is de-facto multi-volume across its constituent communities.

3.2 · Computation — The Killing Form

A dual-coherence analysis of transformer-model internals. 85+ empirical findings across attention algebra, training dynamics, and inference behavior. Key results:

  • Bridge #71 empirical breakthrough — GPU-confirmed P24/P28, 29 predictions validated, Unified Abelian Exception observed.
  • Baseline CV predicts gating — baseline per-layer coefficient of variation predicts the model's gating map at ρ = −0.895, p = 0.0001.
  • Static-mask viability — the predicted gating enables static masking with negligible quality loss; compute savings follow.
  • Dynamic-coherence confirmed — build-dissolve-build oscillation observed at gradient-dialogue level, validating C4 empirically in silicon.

3.3 · Consciousness — Wells

A research instrument for probing structure/observation correspondence in language-model streams. The central quantity is correspondence width W_I := H(S | O) — the residual entropy of structure given the observation. Two independent experiments (Exp 11 and the Qwen 3-condition probe) yield W_I ≈ 0.66 bits, suggesting a rough calibration of the instrument at current resolution. The limit question W_∞ has three formally coherent outcomes: eliminativism, bounded property-dualism, and strict irreducibility. The program is the sequence of instruments that close the gap.

3.4 · Mechanism — The Promethean Configuration

The principle's operational mechanism: carriers break substrate symmetries to actualize content. A carrier acts on a substrate's structural symmetries; content emerges from the symmetry-break in one of two modes.

  • Resolution mode — the substrate carries pre-existing multi-valued content, and the carrier selects a branch. (Quantum measurement, decision-making among formed alternatives, argmax over a learned distribution, voting over pre-existing positions.)
  • Generation mode — the substrate carries pure symmetry, and the carrier actualizes content from the symmetry-break itself. (Gravitational-wave-induced fermion mass generation per Maleknejad-Kopp 2026; creative writing actualizing content the writer didn't pre-form; contemplative practice surfacing experiential content from open attention.) Per A1.3 (Configurational Completeness), generation is local actualization of pre-existing global potential, not creation ex nihilo — the Promethean myth carries this: Prometheus stole fire, did not create it.

The Configuration has three load-bearing claims: necessary (without symmetry-breaking, no content; X stays at maximum symmetry forever), generative (generation mode is primary; resolution is downstream), and recursively reproduced (the same operation recurs at every scale; each layer's content becomes the next layer's symmetry-substrate).

Where to start

Different readers will find different entry points most useful.

  • If you're new to the program — start with The Coherence Principle (Anchor). The first chapter (§1) and the §10 filtering recipe are designed to land without prior context.
  • If you're a philosopher — The Coherence Principle for the formal apparatus; Corpus Perspectival (in development) for the philosophical engagement with neutral monism, panpsychism, phenomenology, Wittgenstein, the hard problem, personal identity, and process philosophy. The SCOPE document outlines the volume's 13 chapters.
  • If you're a physicist — Meridian (cosmology + dark energy prediction) is the primary entry. The Anchor's §1.0 and §5 (descriptive theorem pair) provide the framework's relationship to mathematical description. The Meridian-suite expansion (planned multi-volume) will cover gauge sectors, gravitational dynamics, and matter content separately.
  • If you're an ML researcher — The Killing Form (transformer-internals analysis with 85+ findings) and the Wells consciousness-correspondence-width instrument. The Anchor's C14/C15/C16 (mechanism corollaries) speak directly to training dynamics and intervention design.
  • If you're a clinician or contemplative practitioner — Drift for the lived-practice register, with The Coherent Body, The Coherent Mind, and (planned) A Guide for Coherent Navigation in development. The Anchor's C15 (intervention-at-symmetry-layer) is the framework's primary commitment about applied work.
  • If you want the formal mathematics — Coherent Structure (Companion) is the pure category-theoretic reference. Every formal object the Anchor establishes is developed there with proofs, no "exercise for the reader." The Master Glossary (v0.5) is the cross-Library term reference.
  • If you want to see the program's history — the repository tracks every commit; the CURRENT.md is the live snapshot of where the work stands today.
